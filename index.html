Application for Membership

"At Shelter Island Yacht Club, we are dedicated to fostering a lifelong passion for yachting, from junior sailors to seasoned adults. Rooted in our traditions and rich heritage but forward-looking, our mission is to inspire a sense of fellowship, community, and volunteerism, encouraging connections that transcend sailing and enrich the lives of all our members."

— Shelter Island Yacht Club

This form covers Intermediate (18–35 years old) and Regular (36+ years old) membership classes. The Proposer and Seconder must be Intermediate, Regular, or Senior Members in good standing for no fewer than three years and personally know the applicant(s).

The Application Process

STEP 01
Application submission — The Proposer compiles the completed application, a letter of interest from the candidate, their own supporting letter, the Seconder's letter, and three additional supporting letters from members, and submits all materials as PDF to the Membership Chair.
STEP 02
Committee review — The Membership Committee reviews all submitted materials and schedules an interview. If applying as a couple, both applicants must attend.
STEP 03
Interview — A meeting with the applicant(s) and their Proposer gives the Committee an opportunity to get to know you and answer any questions about the Club.
STEP 04
30-day posting — If the Committee is satisfied, the candidate(s) are posted to the membership for 30 days, subject to space availability.
STEP 05
Board of Trustees review & approval — The application is forwarded to the Board of Trustees for election. The action of the Trustees is the final step in the process.
